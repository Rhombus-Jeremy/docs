---
title: 'Find Cameras by Organization'
description: 'Retrieve all cameras in your organization'
openapi: 'POST /api/camera/findCamerasByOrg'
---

Retrieve a comprehensive list of all cameras deployed in your organization. This endpoint provides details about camera status, configuration, and capabilities.

## Camera Information Returned

- **Device Details**: Model, serial number, firmware version
- **Network Configuration**: IP address, connection status
- **Capabilities**: Resolution, frame rate, night vision
- **Location Data**: Physical location and mounting details
- **Health Status**: Online/offline status, last communication
